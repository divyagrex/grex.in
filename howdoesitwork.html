FAQs

Companies

  1. How long does it take to raise investment on GREX?
    Answer:Private placement regulations stipulate maximum time of 60 days for capital raise. We are trying to minimize the same.
  2. What are the eligibility requirements for a startup to be on GREX?
    Answer:The Company should be growing and progressive with some traction. Reach out to us at startups@grex.in for more details.
  3. Does GREX takes equity in startups that raise funds through its platform?
    Answer: No.
  4. How is a startup valued on GREX?
    Answer:We seek constant feedback from startup, sponsor and investors interested in the company and then arrive at an acceptable valuation.
  5. Can a company choose its sponsor?
    Answer: Yes.

Investors

  1. How does GREX ensure that good companies come on the platform?
    Answer:Companies need to go through the following process before getting listed on GREX:
    1. Sponsor first checks whether the company is ready to raise capital. If not, sponsor works with the company on key areas of improvement before it is taken on to the platform.
    2. Once the sponsor approves that company, it goes through the GREX internal team for approval.
    3. After GREX internal team's approval, a company selection committee approves the selected companies for final listing on the platform.
    4. After listing, a company needs to be on the platform for at least 3 months before it can raise capital on the platform.
    5. A company needs to get a grading before it can trigger capital raise on the platform.
  2. Will I get updates about my companies that I invest through GREX?
    Answer:Yes, regular updates about companies listed on the platform will be shared.
  3. Is it possible to sell my shares on GREX?
    Answer:Yes, GREX allows secondary sale of its listed companies.
  4. What is the minimum period I need to hold shares in GREX companies?
    Answer:There is no minimum period.
  5. I own equity in small growing companies. Can I bring them on GREX?
    Answer:Yes, you can reach out to GREX or one of our sponsors to know more.
  6. I am a Non Resident Indian. Can I invest in GREX companies?
    Answer:Yes, drop us a mail at invest@grex.in for more details.
  7. I am a Venture Capital company. Can I invest through GREX?
    Answer:Yes, drop us a mail at invest@grex.in for more details.
  8. What is the minimum investment required to invest in GREX companies?
    Answer:It will vary from company to company. Please login to know more.

Sponsors

  1. Who is a Sponsor?
    Answer:A sponsor is a legal entity (company or LLP) that handholds the companies to make them capital ready. Sponsors are grass root players, having helped companies raise capital. They also bring deep financial knowledge along with other statutory capabilities.
  2. What are the responsibilities of a Sponsor?
    Answer:A sponsor ensures that right companies come on platform and their information is continuously updated on the platform. It also ensures that the best corporate governance practices are followed at companies, apart from managing investors of its portfolio companies.
  3. We are a small VC firm. Can we be a Sponsor?
    Answer:Yes, reach out to us to know more.
  4. Can there be multiple sponsors per company?
    Answer: No.
  5. We are a small Angel Network. Can we become Sponsor on GREX?
    Answer:Yes, reach out to us to know more.
  6. Does GREX mandate sponsor and company terms?
    Answer:No. GREX is a process driven technology platform that provides guidelines but it does not mandate any terms.
